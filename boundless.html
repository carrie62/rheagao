• About

    Boundless is a magazine that will talk about life and life-style in general. It will be heavily editorial with articles ranging from health to travelling the world, including philantropy; financial, social and political issues, as well as fashion and arts.

    Audience:
    The main demographic is canadians, all genders 45+. Secondary demographic: canadians, all genders 25-45, post-secondary education (undergraduate and graduate degrees), urban setting.
